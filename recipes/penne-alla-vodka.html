Penne Alla Vodka

Description

Penne alla Vodka is a super easy pasta recipe that'll leave you licking your plate clean! It's made with penne pasta tossed in a creamy tomato vodka sauce with garlic and pancetta. If a big bowl of comfort food is what you're looking for then this is it!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup smoked pancetta cubed (100g)
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 4 cups penne pasta (14oz / 400g)
  • 1.5 cups puréed tomatoes (passata) (400g)
  • 1/3 cups heavy cream (80ml)
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water (120ml)
  • salt and pepper to season
  • freshly grated parmesan for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted boiling water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to packet instruction until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, add the olive oil to a large pan and add the pancetta, fry for a couple of minutes until starting to brown (2-3 minutes)
  3. Next, add the garlic and shallot and saute until the shallot becomes translucent and garlic is fragrant.
  4. Stir the pureed tomatoes into the skillet and add ½ cup of reserved pasta water. Let it simmer for 1 minute then reduce the heat to low.
  5. Add the cream and stir until thoroughly combined with the tomato. Once combined, add the vodka with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for 3-4 minutes or until you can no longer smell the vodka and the sauce has darkened slightly. If the sauce reduces too much then add another splash of pasta water.
  7. Once the pasta is cooked al dente, add it to the sauce and toss to coat. Serve with parmesan.
